What is Social Engineering

Social engineering is the manipulation of a person, rather than a system, to gain unauthorized access to information. Attackers rely on triggers such as urgency, authority, fear, and curiosity.

Infographic placeholder: the four common phishing indicators (urgency, authority, fear, curiosity).

Key indicators to watch for: mismatched sender address, generic greeting, urgent requests, suspicious links, and requests for sensitive data.
